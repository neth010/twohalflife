The Cosmological Lithium Problem Identified

Potential Explanations Evaluated

Breaking down why the theoretical simulation values drastically exceed real-world data bounds.

Observation Systematics

Unknown structural biases or atmospheric profiling inaccuracies hidden within ancient halo star data models.

Stellar Physics (Depletion)

Uniform in-situ depletion and burning of delicate lithium layers carried into deep hot zones across stellar interiors over cosmic timelines.

Nuclear Physics Rates

Potential systemic cross-section math errors in the underlying thermonuclear rates that govern Lithium-7/Beryllium-7 synthesis channels.

Beyond the Standard Model

Exotic physics solutions (e.g., decaying dark matter loops). However, these extensions historically corrupt the highly sensitive, matching Deuterium yield caps.

Central Inquiry: Can undiscovered nuclear physics reaction rate variations account for all observed primordial parameters?
